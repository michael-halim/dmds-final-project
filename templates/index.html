Data Modeling for Data Science Project

Project Task

Develop an OLAP app that employs at least 2 databases to store and retrieve data

  • Define at least 3 (three) problems addressed by the app
  • One of the DBs must be a NoSQL (e.g., Redis, MongoDB, Neo4j, etc.)
  • The DBs comprise at least 5 (five) entities / tables / docs
  • Make sure that the DBs are employed correctly according to their characteristics
